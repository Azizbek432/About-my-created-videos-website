# 🎬 About My Created Videos Website

Ushbu loyiha mening ijodiy ishlarim — men yaratgan videolar, darsliklar va vizual kontentlarni bir joyga jamlagan interaktiv platformadir. Sayt foydalanuvchilarga men tomonidan tayyorlangan media mahsulotlarni qulay tartibda ko'rish imkonini beradi.



## 🚀 Jonli demo (Live Demo)
Videolarni ko'rish uchun: [about-my-created-videos-website.vercel.app](https://about-my-created-videos-website.vercel.app/)

---

## 🇺🇿 Loyiha haqida
Bu sayt shunchaki video galereya emas, balki shaxsiy raqamli arxivdir. Loyihani yaratishda asosiy e'tibor media elementlarning tez yuklanishi, toza interfeys va foydalanuvchiga qulay navigatsiya yaratishga qaratilgan.

### ✨ Asosiy xususiyatlari:
* **Video Showcase:** Har bir video uchun maxsus kartochkalar va sarlavhalar.
* **Responsive Video Gallery:** Videolar barcha qurilmalarda (telefon, planshet) to'g'ri nisbatda ko'rinadi.
* **Modern UI:** Minimalistik va zamonaviy dizayn elementlari.
* **Vercel Fast Hosting:** Videolar va sahifa yuklanish tezligi optimallashgan.

---

## 🇬🇧 About the Project
This website is a dedicated platform to showcase my video creations, tutorials, and digital content. It serves as a personal media hub where visitors can explore my creative work in a well-organized and aesthetically pleasing environment.

### ✨ Key Features:
* **Optimized Media Display:** High-quality video previews and seamless playback experience.
* **Clean & Minimalist Design:** Focuses on content rather than distracting elements.
* **Fully Responsive:** Adapts perfectly to various screen resolutions and sizes.
* **One-Click Access:** Direct links to original sources or embedded players.

## 🛠 Texnologiyalar | Technologies
* **HTML5** — Video teglari va semantik struktura uchun.
* **CSS3** — Galereya dizayni, hover effektlari va moslashuvchanlik uchun.
